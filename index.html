About Me

I'm Eduar Padilla, originally from Honduras, and I moved to the United States when I was about 8. My interests are, philosophy, psychology, history, and technology, with a special focus on the latter. This interest began when I was young, sparked by my love for video games and my dream of creating my own. As I've explored technology more deeply, my fascination has only increased, seeing its major impact on society and its potential for significant change. I'm especially captivated by artificial intelligence and medical technology, amazed by their rapid growth and their crucial role in building our global society.
